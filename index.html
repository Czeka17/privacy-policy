Landmark Legends - privacy policy

Last updated: 11.09.2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard non-personal information collected through our React Native mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect non-personal information through our App, which includes:

  • Completed Levels: We store information about the levels you have completed within the App.
  • Hints: We store hints you may have used within the App.
  • Time: Time till you can again use free daily hint.
  • Last bonus level: Last level that gave you hints.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your gaming experience by providing information about completed levels and hints you've used.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored in Async Storage. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of this page.
